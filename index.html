Academic Projects


1. Computational Analysis of Jane Eyre (N-gram)

Course: Computational English Linguistics (Prof. Ahn)
Tools: R, Python
Description: This project analyzes the linguistic patterns in Charlotte Brontë's Jane Eyre using computational methods. I conducted an N-gram analysis to identify frequently used word clusters and studied the stylistic evolution of the characters.

  • Pre-processed raw text data (tokenization, stop-word removal).
  • Visualized word frequency and relationships using R.


2. University English Textbook Design

Role: Layout & Cover Designer
Description: Collaborated with a professor to design a university-level English textbook. My focus was on creating a user-friendly layout that enhances readability for students and designing an aesthetic cover that reflects the book's academic tone.



3. The Evolution of Heroic Narratives

Topic: Gothic Literature & Character Analysis
Description: A comparative study on the protagonists of Frankenstein and modern dystopian novels. Explored how the definition of a 'hero' shifts depending on the societal fears of the era.
